1) Wait for the pod:

     kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} rollout status statefulset/{{ include "mailnite.fullname" . }}

2) Forward the setup console to your machine and open it:

     kubectl -n {{ .Release.Namespace }} port-forward svc/{{ include "mailnite.fullname" . }} 8480:8480

     →  http://localhost:8480

   First run walks you through everything: console password → storage →
   admin account → infrastructure (domains, DNS, webmail, TLS, ports).
   On the wizard's last step, KEEP the service panel — on Kubernetes,
   port-forward to 8480 is the natural admin path.

   Two Ports-step notes for Kubernetes:
     • keep the HIGH internal binds (2525/2465/2587/2993) — the chart's mail
       Service maps the standard ports onto them at the edge;
     • enable the mail Service when ready:
         helm upgrade {{ .Release.Name }} <chart> --reuse-values --set service.mail.enabled=true
